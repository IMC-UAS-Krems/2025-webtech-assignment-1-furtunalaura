Little description of myself:

I am currently a student at IMC Krems, studying Informatics in English. I graduated from high school in 2025 in Moldova and spent an exchange semester in Brussels, where I studied in French. This international experience helped me adapt to diverse environments and improved my language and communication skills.

My interest in programming began after taking some courses and internationally accredited exams, which provided me with a broader understanding of what computer science truly encompasses. Beyond technology, I am also passionate about economics, finance, and accounting, as I enjoy learning how these fields connect with innovation and global development. My hobbies include learning new languages, cycling, hiking, skiing, and trying new recipes.
